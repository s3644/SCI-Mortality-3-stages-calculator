⚕️ Clinical Note
This model was trained on NSCISC 2021 data (35,631 patients, 34% mortality, mean follow-up 13.3 years). Predictions are Cox proportional hazards risk estimates. The model uses 85 engineered features with native N/A handling. Research use only — not for clinical decision-making without prospective validation.
